Cookies Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

This Cookies Policy explains how Horizon Crypto ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website (horizoncrypto.com) or use our services.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit (language preference, login status, analytics, etc.).

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies – necessary for the website to function (authentication, security, basic navigation)
  • Performance & analytics cookies – help us understand how visitors use the site (Google Analytics, Hotjar)
  • Functional cookies – remember your preferences (dark mode, language, currency)
  • Marketing & advertising cookies – used to show relevant ads and measure campaign performance

3. Third-party cookies

We use services provided by third parties that may set cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics & Google Tag Manager
  • Cloudflare (security & performance)
  • Intercom (customer support chat)
  • Meta Pixel (advertising measurement)

4. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all sites
  • Receive a warning before a cookie is stored

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our platform.

5. Cookie consent

When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie banner giving you the option to accept or customize your cookie preferences. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.
