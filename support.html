Popular Questions

How long do crypto withdrawals take?

Most cryptocurrency withdrawals are processed within 10–40 minutes depending on network congestion. Bitcoin may take longer during high-fee periods. Fiat withdrawals via bank transfer usually take 1–3 business days.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive a secure reset link. If you also have 2FA enabled, you'll need access to your authenticator app or backup codes.
