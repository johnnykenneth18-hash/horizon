Cryptocurrency and digital asset investments carry a high level of risk and may not be suitable for all investors. You could lose all or a substantial portion of your capital. Horizon Crypto strongly advises you to read this entire disclosure and consult with independent financial advisors before committing funds.

Market volatility risk

  • Extreme price fluctuations: Digital asset prices can change rapidly and unpredictably, moving double digits in short periods. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
  • Market manipulation & sentiment: The crypto market is susceptible to social media influence, "whale" transactions, and unanticipated regulatory news that may cause sudden drops.
  • Liquidity risk: Some tokens may become illiquid, making it difficult to exit positions at fair price, especially during market stress.

Regulatory & legal risk

  • Changing laws: The regulatory landscape for cryptocurrencies is evolving. New laws or interpretations in jurisdictions where Horizon Crypto operates (Australia, Switzerland, UK, Brazil, etc.) may adversely affect platform functionality or asset values.
  • Tax treatment: Gains from crypto investments may be subject to capital gains tax, VAT, or other levies. Clients are solely responsible for understanding their tax obligations.
  • Licence conditions: While Horizon Crypto holds multiple licences, changes in regulatory requirements could impact the services we provide.

Technology & security risks

  • Blockchain vulnerabilities: Smart contract bugs, consensus attacks (e.g. 51% attacks), or network forks could lead to loss of funds.
  • Custody & cyber threats: Despite industry-leading security measures, no system is immune to hacking, phishing, or internal breaches. Assets held with Horizon Crypto are not covered by government insurance schemes (e.g. FDIC).
  • Operational disruptions: Technical failures, maintenance, or force majeure events may temporarily suspend trading or access to funds.

Platform & investment risks

  • No guaranteed returns: Any projected returns or past performance shown on our marketing materials are for illustration only. Actual results may differ materially.
  • Leverage / derivatives risk: If you use margin or derivative products, losses can exceed deposited capital. These products are only suitable for experienced investors.
  • Counterparty risk: Some strategies may involve third-party custodians or DeFi protocols; failure of those parties could result in total loss.

Client responsibilities

  • Independent advice: You acknowledge that you have sufficient knowledge and experience to understand the risks, or you have sought independent advice.
  • Account security: You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and enabling two-factor authentication.
  • Tax and legal compliance: You bear all responsibility for reporting and paying any taxes due on your investments.
